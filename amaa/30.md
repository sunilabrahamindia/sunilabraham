The **30th Death Anniversary** of Reverend [A. M. A. Ayrookuzhiel](/amaa/) will be on Sunday, 29 November 2026. One of the most important objectives of the Sunil Abraham Project is to document the biography and works of A. M. A. Ayrookuzhiel. This event page documents the plans, progress, and details related to the commemoration.

In the coming months, we will be working on related content and articles. Version 3.0 of the Sunil Abraham Project will launch on Sunday, 29 November 2026. An in-person event and exhibition will also be held in Bengaluru, Karnataka on the same day.

A bulletin will be published monthly on the 15th of every month, from April through Sunday, 15 November 2026. Each bulletin will carry news and updates related to the event, alongside stories and anecdotes from A. M. A. Ayrookuzhiel's life.


## Background

The Sunil Abraham Project was started on Sunday, 2 October 2025. From the beginning we have been working on content related to A. M. A. Ayrookuzhiel with the highest priority. Our first featured article showcased on the main page was A. M. A. Ayrookuzhiel's biography. In November 2025, during the 29th death anniversary month, we started [Portal:A. M. A. Ayrookuzhiel](/amaa/portal/).

For the upcoming 30th death anniversary, [Sunil Abraham](/sunil/), the founder of the Sunil Abraham Project, along with his brothers and family members, visioned to make a larger and more tributeful commemoration this year. On Saturday, 29 November 2025, on the 29th death anniversary, Sunil Abraham posted on his social media accounts:

> "In preparation for the 30th anniversary next year, .... started gathering his life and writings in a new digital portal: [https://sunilabraham.in/amaa/portal/](https://sunilabraham.in/amaa/portal/). We are hoping that this will be a comprehensive archive of all his research outputs and also a collection of personal artifacts that may be of interest to colleagues, friends and family."

This event page will document the planning and progress of the commemoration, including tasks, timelines, and responsible persons; regular bulletins published roughly every 30 days from April through November 2026; digital and physical activities planned around the anniversary; and any publications, tributes, or archival additions made in the lead-up to 29 November 2026.
