This page lists DOI-backed archival editions and preserved research publications archived on [Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/), the open-access research repository operated by CERN and OpenAIRE.

The TSAP website functions as a living and continuously evolving knowledge platform. Zenodo archival editions preserve stable, citable snapshots of selected publications, reports, and historical reconstructions for long-term scholarly reference and preservation.

## Students for Peace (Bangalore, 1993)

**Version:** 1.0  
**Published:** 18 May 2026  
**DOI:** [10.5281/zenodo.20272980](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20272980)

Archival edition documenting the Students for Peace initiative organised in Bangalore in February 1993 in response to communal violence following the demolition of the Babri Masjid. Prepared using surviving planning documents, contemporary newspaper coverage, campaign materials, and preserved organisational records.
