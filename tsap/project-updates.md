The **Project Updates** archive records significant milestones, achievements, announcements, and developments related to The Sunil Abraham Project (TSAP). Its purpose is to preserve a chronological record of notable events in the life of the project and to document major moments in its growth, preservation, and public presentation.

Since the launch of The Sunil Abraham Project on 2 October 2025, important developments have occasionally been highlighted through notices displayed in the website footer. These updates typically marked major achievements such as page-count milestones, new features, preservation initiatives, infrastructure improvements, or other noteworthy developments.

During the early stages of the project, no dedicated archive existed for these announcements. Some milestones were recorded within broader historical documentation, including the project's timeline available on the [The Sunil Abraham Project](/articles/sunil-abraham-project/#timeline/) page, while others appeared only temporarily in the footer and were not systematically archived.

In June 2026, the number and frequency of project announcements increased. For the first time, multiple updates were displayed simultaneously within the footer's update section, highlighting the need for a dedicated archival record. This led to the creation of the Project Updates archive, which serves as a permanent archive for important announcements and milestones associated with TSAP.

**Notes:** Entries are arranged chronologically and are intended to document significant developments in the history of The Sunil Abraham Project. New entries should normally be added near the top of the archive so that recent updates remain easy to locate while preserving the historical record of earlier milestones.

Each entry should indicate whether that was featured in the website footer, on a dedicated project page, or through another communication channel.
