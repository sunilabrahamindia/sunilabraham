**The Sunil Abraham Project (TSAP)** photographic archive workspace continues the systematic organisation of images relating to Sunil Abraham, his family, and associated personal and institutional histories.

This page builds on earlier entries and forms part of an ongoing effort to identify, describe, and contextualise photographic material across the project. The archive is being developed incrementally, with information added as it becomes available.

Entries below remain in a draft state. Descriptive details, including dates, locations, and identification of individuals, are being added progressively. Many of these images will also be used within relevant articles across the site.

This page is publicly accessible as part of the project's working documentation.
