Sunil Abraham has spoken at, presented in, moderated, trained at, or participated in a wide range of national and international events on digital rights, privacy, technology policy, free and open-source software, media governance, and internet governance. This page lists these events chronologically, starting from the earliest available records.

## 2001
- 27–30 January 2001: Advocacy in the Internet Age: Way Forward for Civil Society from South Asia (Bangalore). Sunil Abraham participated in this multi-day civil society consultation.
- 10–11 July 2001: Use of Information and Communications Technologies for Social Empowerment and Economic Development (Bangalore). Sunil Abraham participated in discussions on ICTs and development.

## 2003
- 2–8 November 2003: Workshop for Asian Women Journalists (Bangalore). Sunil Abraham contributed to training sessions and programme discussions. 

## 2005
- 18 November 2005: Panel Discussion on Free and Open Source Software at the World Summit for the Information Society (Tunis). Sunil Abraham participated as a panelist in this international discussion.
- 14–20 November 2005: World Information City (Bangalore). Sunil Abraham participated in dialogues on technology, culture and public spaces.
- 1–4 September 2005: Free/Open Source Software – Asia Pacific II (Cambodia). Sunil Abraham took part in sessions on FOSS adoption and policy.
- 28 January – 5 February 2005: Asia Source I (Bangalore). Sunil Abraham participated in workshops and training programmes on FOSS technologies.

## 2006
- 2 and 4 May 2006: Regional Policy Dialogue on Open Standards (Bangkok). Sunil Abraham participated in regional discussions on standards for digital governance.
- 6–8 June 2006: Asia Commons (Bangkok). Sunil Abraham took part in panels on knowledge sharing, open content and commons-based development.

## 2007
- 5 November 2007: FOSS and the Indian Government (Delhi). Sunil Abraham participated in a session organised by the Bureau of Police Research and Development, Government of India.
- 2–3 December 2007: Design of the Times (DOTT07) (Newcastle upon Tyne, United Kingdom). Sunil Abraham participated as part of the programme organised by the Design Council and regional partners.
- 1 October 2007: FOSS and the Indian Government (Delhi). Sunil Abraham contributed to discussions organised by the Ministry of Home Affairs, CRPF, BSF and ITBP.
